Justice our foundation

Founded in 1834. Chartered in 1901.

Founded in 1834 at Williams College, Delta Upsilon is the seventh oldest fraternity in North America. For over 180 years, Delta Upsilon has maintained a commitment to its Four Founding Principles:
  • Promotion of Friendship
  • Development of Character
  • Diffusion of Liberal Culture
  • Advancement of Justice
This charge challenges our brothers to cultivate what is finest in themselves and others; to go beyond the familiar and discover goodness in all corners of life.
